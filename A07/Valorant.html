Welcome to Valorant

Valorant is a free-to-play tactical shooter by Riot Games. Compete in high-stakes matches with unique agents and strategic gameplay.

Dive into the world of agents, weapons, and maps and sharpen your skills to rise through the ranks.

Release Date

Valorant was officially released on June 2, 2020. It quickly became a top-tier tactical shooter due to its strategic gameplay and hero-based mechanics.
