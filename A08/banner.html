Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

Release Date: December 14, 2018

Directors: Bob Persichetti, Peter Ramsey, Rodney Rothman

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse follows Miles Morales, a teenager from Brooklyn, as he becomes Spider-Man in his own universe. After being bitten by a radioactive spider, Miles discovers he has spider-like powers. Things take a dramatic turn when he meets alternate versions of Spider-Man from different dimensions. The multiverse is collapsing due to a collider built by the villain Kingpin (Wilson Fisk), who is trying to bring back his deceased wife and son from another universe.
